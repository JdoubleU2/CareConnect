About Our Chatbot

CareConnect is an AI-powered health assistant designed to help users better understand medical symptoms, conditions, and general health concerns. By using natural language processing, our chatbot engages in real-time conversation to provide clear explanations of medical terms, suggest potential causes of symptoms, and guide users toward appropriate next steps.

Whether you're a student researching a condition, a parent with questions about a child's symptoms, or someone just looking for trustworthy information, CareConnect offers accessible support—anytime, anywhere.

It’s important to note that CareConnect does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is intended to complement—not substitute—the guidance of licensed healthcare providers.
